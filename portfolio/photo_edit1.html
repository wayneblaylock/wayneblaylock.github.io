Removing the Hiker in a Photo

This was a fun project to work on. There isn’t any background information on a job this was part of, this was just a personal project to practice my photo editing skills. I came across this picture of a hiker in-front of a beautiful landscape.

I wanted to see how it would look without the hiker in it, so I got to work. I cloned different parts of the photo to lay the landscape over the hiker. Next I blended it together to remove seams and to make the changes look natural. Finally I did some small touch ups, and decided to leave the colors and lighting the way they were.

In the end, I was pretty happy with how it turned out. I could have worked on it some more to perfect all the details of the photo, but I didn’t want to spend any more time on it since this wasn't going to be used for anything.
