Changing the Background and Feel of a Photo

While looking for photos to practice my editing skills on, I came across this cool picture of a long road in front of a mountainous terrain. This picture would work great for many uses, but it may not capture the feel required for many other projects.

I loved the layout of the picture, but felt the tone was a little down and dreary. I decided to change a couple of things to give it a warm atmosphere and totally different feel. First, I wanted to replace the gray cloudy sky with something brighter. I found that a mountain peak was hidden in fog, so I put the mountain back in the picture. Next I found a picture of a sky that I liked, and made the switch. Finally, I tweaked the colors of the original image to better fit the lighting and feel of the new sky, and just like that I was done!

This variation of the picture creates a new atmosphere, and opens new possibilities for uses. In the end, I was happy with the way it turned out.
